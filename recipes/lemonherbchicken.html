Simple Lemon Herb Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 5oz skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 medium lemon, juiced, divided
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tb olive oil
  • 1 pinch dried oregano
  • 2 sprigs fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Cooking Directions

  1. Place chicken in bowl; pour half of the lemon juice over chicken and season with salt.

  2. Heat olive oil in medium skillet over medium-low heat.

    Place chicken into hot oil

    Add remaining lemon juice and oregano; season with black pepper.

    Cook chicken until golden brown and the juices run clear, 5-10 mins per side.

    An instant read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F.

  3. Garnish the chicken with parsley to serve.
